About Alexandra

My career in the environmental sector has taught me that well designed software and web apps translate to mission accomplished. From mobile apps that allow volunteers to enter data or report unusual conditions to web apps that notify the public of environmental hazards in real time, to cloud based databases to track donors, the right technology makes all the difference.

My interest in web technologies led me to pursue a certificate in web development. This training combined with experience as Director of Communications at a local non-profit has provided me with a unique perspective and real world experience. In every project I strive to build user-friendly, interactive applications with powerful impact.

I graduated with honors from the University of Chicago in 2008 with a BA in Public Policy.
